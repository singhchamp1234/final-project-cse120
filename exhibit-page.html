Exhibit Title: 1492: An Ongoing Voyage


Dates Running: August 13, 2019-August 30, 2019


About: Columbus. The date and the name provoke many questions related to the linking of very different parts of the world, the Western Hemisphere and the Mediterranean. What was life like in those areas before 1492? What spurred European expansion? How did European, African and American peoples react to each other? What were some of the immediate results of these contacts?
